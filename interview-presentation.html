💡 The Solution: My Approach

1

Foundation & Setup

Configured Shopify platform with custom domain, payment gateway integration, and multi-region shipping. Launched with 3 core products and established brand identity: "Style. Sip. Step." Implemented comprehensive analytics from day one to track all user behavior.

2

Product Scaling & Organization

Expanded catalog 266% from 3 to 11 products across 9 diverse categories (Fashion, Home, Tech, Wellness). Created 8-collection architecture with tag-based navigation system. Developed SEO-optimized descriptions and strategic pricing.

3

Optimization & Conversion

Implemented conversion optimization tactics: streamlined checkout, trust signals, clear shipping info, and mobile responsiveness. Achieved 3.9% add-to-cart rate through data-driven page optimization and strategic product placement.

4

Traffic & Results

Generated 77+ sessions across 3 geographic locations (US, India). Processed 3 successful orders achieving 1.3% conversion rate. Documented 24% session growth and established foundation for email marketing and mobile optimization.
